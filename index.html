State of Vermont Requirements - Members and Guests

  1. Non-Vermonters: Quarantining before entry to the site must be done unless you have documented vaccine completion more than 14 days prior
  2. Everyone: Masks must be worn when inside buildings and within 6 feet of others outdoors
  3. Everyone: You must sign-in in the pavilion upon entry to the site
